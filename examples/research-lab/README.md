# Research Lab Example

An AI research lab with 6 agents investigating LLM reasoning and memory capabilities.

## Team Structure

```
Principal Investigator
├── Reasoning Research Lead
│   └── Reasoning Experiment Runner
├── Memory Research Lead
│   └── Memory Experiment Runner
└── Peer Reviewer
```

## Research Tracks

- **Reasoning benchmarks** (40% budget) — Chain-of-thought experiments across model families
- **Memory architecture** (35% budget) — RAG vs fine-tuned vs hybrid retention
- **Reserve** (25%) — Ad-hoc experiments, literature review

## How It Works

1. **PI** wakes up, reviews progress across both research tracks, allocates compute budget
2. **Research Leads** design experiments, break them into tasks, assign to experiment runners
3. **Experiment Runners** execute experiments, collect data, attach results as evidence
4. **Peer Reviewer** reviews completed experiments for methodological rigor (verifier gate)
5. **Memory review** extracts experimental findings to knowledge base automatically

## Why Clawforce For Research

- **Budget enforcement** — $80/day compute budget prevents runaway experiments
- **Evidence requirements** — Experiment results must be attached before marking complete
- **Peer review gate** — Reviewer must approve before results are accepted
- **Initiative tracking** — See spend and progress per research track
- **Memory lifecycle** — Experimental findings automatically promoted to team knowledge
- **Reproducibility** — Full audit trail of every experiment, parameters, and outcomes
